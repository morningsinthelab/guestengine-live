## Bio

EJ "Skullcrusher" Snyder is a 25-year decorated Army combat veteran, extreme survivalist, Discovery Channel star, serial entrepreneur, motivational speaker, and author. Based in Raeford, North Carolina, EJ has built a life defined by an absolute refusal to quit — a principle forged on the battlefield, tested in the most extreme survival environments on television, and now applied to building businesses and inspiring men to find their purpose after service.

EJ's military career began in 1984 when he enlisted in the United States Army at age 19. Over the next quarter-century, he rose from private to Sergeant Major (E-9) — the highest enlisted rank in the Army. He served as an Airborne Ranger in elite infantry units, completed two combat tours (Gulf War and Iraq), and earned two Bronze Stars and the Legion of Merit for exceptional service. He served as a Ranger Instructor at the U.S. Army Ranger School in Florida for three years, training the next generation of Army Rangers in the most demanding military leadership course in the world. He later served as a primary survival instructor and attended Man Tracking School, learning to track men and beasts on the ground. After retiring, he transitioned to the U.S. Army SERE (Survival, Evasion, Resistance, Escape) school, working alongside like-minded veterans — a move he credits with helping him successfully navigate the transition to civilian life.

After his military career, EJ channeled his survival expertise into television. He became the most decorated contestant in the history of Discovery Channel's Naked and Afraid franchise, completing six challenges totaling over 206 days in the most brutal environments on earth — with zero tap-outs. He earned the title of "Godfather of Naked and Afraid" and is recognized as the number one ranked survivalist in the world by The Top Tens. His television career expanded to include hosting Season 9 of Dual Survival, appearing on Ed Stafford's First Man Out, 72 Hours, Mountain Masters, Lost, and the History Channel's Patton 360. During one legendary 60-day challenge in a Louisiana swamp, EJ suffered a severe scrotal injury and received eight stitches with no anesthesia and no antibiotics — then refused to leave, getting up to gather firewood before the sun went down. The story has become one of the most iconic moments in survival television history.

EJ is now a serial entrepreneur with multiple active ventures. He is the Executive Vice President and co-founder of Freedom Pickles USA Inc., a veteran-owned premium pickle brand built on his partner's grandmother's secret recipe. Freedom Pickles uses all-natural, locally sourced, organic ingredients with no preservatives, no dyes, no GMOs, and no hardening agents — scoring a 90 on the Yuka nutrition app, making it one of the healthiest pickles on the market. The brand launched with three bold flavors (Patriot Dill, Gunny's Garlicky, and Firework Frenzy) and is expanding into big-box retail stores. EJ also founded Skullcrusher Outdoors and Rocko Ventures LLC, is a major shareholder and brand ambassador for Armed Forces Brewing Company (co-owned by Rob O'Neill, the Navy SEAL who killed Bin Laden), serves as EVP and Chief Instructor for Survival Mastery — a subscriber-based online survival education platform — and is involved with Freedom 75, a wellness facility for veterans and first responders.

EJ is the published author of the Emergency Home Preparedness Guide, which became a number one bestseller on Amazon across five categories. He is a sought-after motivational speaker whose message centers on the warrior mindset: finding your purpose, refusing to be a victim, and building the mental toughness to push through any obstacle. His personal philosophy — 'If I can't get around it, over it, or under it, I'm going through it' — was forged through childhood bullying, military service, and 206 days naked in the wild. He speaks openly about PTSD, the challenges of military-to-civilian transition, and the importance of finding a new mission after service. With over 215,000 followers on Facebook alone and a growing presence across Instagram, TikTok, X, and YouTube (Survive with EJ), EJ Snyder has become one of the most authentic and respected voices in the survival, veteran, and men's personal development space.
