PROVENANCE & CONTROL

YOU SET THE BOUNDARIES. YOU OWN THE IP.

Every Twin runs under explicit consent and a content-rules engine. Four rule types govern what your Twin will and won’t do. You can update or revoke any time.

Can Discuss

Topics your Twin engages with. Pulled from your conversation map but you can add or remove.

Cannot Discuss

Topics permanently off-limits — legal matters, family details, anything you want kept private.

Always Mention

Required mentions every time the topic comes up — your book, your business, your cause.

Never Position

Roles or framings your Twin will refuse — e.g. “not a financial advisor,” “not a medical professional.”
